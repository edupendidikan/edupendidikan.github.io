1. cara membedakan past form dengan past continuous form


Jawaban:

Pertama, pahami pengertiannya :

Simple past tense = menyatakan bahwa suatu aksi yang dilakukan atau terjadi pada masa yang lampau.

Past continuous tense = menyatakan bahwa suatu aksi sedang terjadi pada waktu tertentu di masa lampau. Aksi tersebut telah dimulai tapi belum selesai pada saat itu.

Sudah tau bedanya belum? Jadi, kalau past continuous itu untuk memberitahu suatu aksi yang terjadi selama momen tertentu di masa lampau, misal : I was shopping at this time yesterday.

Nah, biasanya, past tense dan continuous tense juga bisa digunakan dalam satu kalimat. Jika hal ini terjadi, maka biasanya past continuous yg menjelaskan lebih panjang ttg background dr peristiwa tsb, lalu past tense yg menjelaskan kejadian atau peristiwa nya..

Contoh : When I woke up this morning it was raining and my father was singing in the kitchen. 

Kalimat "I woke up this morning" merupakan simple past tense

Kalimat "It was raining and my father was singing" merupakan past continuous tense.

Past tense or past form is to state about the activities or event in the past. The pattern is : S + V2 + complement.
For the V2, some verbs are irregular, which change completely or irregularly
like hit, make, eat while some other verbs are regular, which only needs adding -ed at the end of it like play, decide, call.

22. 3. write 10 words in past form ( simple past tense)​


Simple Past Tense selalu menggunakan verb 2 atau, verb bentuk past. Biasanya identik dengan penambahan huruf -d atau -ed di belakangnya.

Seperti: Passed, worked, cleaned

Tapi banyak juga, verb yang tidak bisa ditambah huruf2 tersebut atau bentuknya paten (verb 2 sudah ditentukan dari sananya)

Seperti: Woke, got, drove, made, came

Dan biasanya, dalam bentuk kalimat past (lampau atau telah terjadi), ada penanda waktu seperti: yesterday, last night, last month, dll

Contoh kalimat:

1. I woke up at 6 a.m yesterday

2. I came to your house

3. I passed that street last night

4. She worked here before

5. He got that achievement last week

6. They made a delicious cake last month

7. She slept at 8 p.m last night

8. I drove his car yesterday

9. I went to his school last sunday

10. I gave him some money yesterday

Rumus Past Tense

Past Simple :

(+) S + verb 2 + O

(-) S + did not + verb 1 + O

(?) Did + S + verb 1 + O + ?

Past Continuous :

(+) S + was/were + Verb ing + O

(-) S + was/were + not + Verb ing + O

(?) Was/were + S + Verb ing + O

was : untuk i, he, she, it

were : untuk you, they, we
