7. Quiz Note:Heart is breaking correct answer Tentukan Akar-akar persamaan -2x²-7x-6=0​


x₁ = -3/2 dan x₂ = -2

Pembahasan:

-2x² – 7x – 6 = 0

2x² + 7x + 6 = 0

(2x + 3)(x + 2) = 0

2x + 3 = 0ㅤ x + 2 = 0

ㅤ 2x = -3ㅤㅤㅤx = -2

ㅤㅤx = -3/2

∴ Jadi x₁ = -3/2 dan x₂ = -2
