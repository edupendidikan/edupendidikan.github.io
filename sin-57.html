3. Nilai dari sin 57 adalah


Jawab:

[tex]\displaystyle \frac{2\left ( \sqrt{3}+1 \right )\sqrt{5+\sqrt{5}}-\sqrt{2}\left ( \sqrt{3}-1 \right )\left ( \sqrt{5}-1 \right )}{16}[/tex]

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Cari sin 18°

x = 18° → 5x = 90°

2x + 3x = 90°

sin 2x = sin (90° - 3x)

2 sin x cos x = cos 3x

2 sin x cos x = 4 cos³ x - 3 cos x

2 sin x = 4 cos² x - 3

2 sin x = 4(1 - sin² x) - 3

2 sin x = -4 sin² x + 1

4 sin² x + 2 sin x - 1 = 0

[tex]\begin{aligned}\sin x&=\frac{-2\pm\sqrt{2^2-4(4)(-1)}}{2(4)}\\\sin 18^\circ&=\frac{-1+\sqrt{5}}{4}\end{aligned}[/tex]

Ambil yang positif karena di kuadran I

Cari cos 18°

[tex]\begin{aligned}\cos 18^\circ&=\sqrt{1-\sin^2 18^\circ}\\&=\sqrt{1-\left ( \frac{\sqrt{5}-1}{4} \right )^2}\\&=\sqrt{1-\frac{6-2\sqrt{5}}{16}}\\&=\sqrt{\frac{10+2\sqrt{5}}{16}}\\&=\frac{\sqrt{2\left ( 5+\sqrt{5} \right )}}{4}\\&=\frac{\sqrt{2}~\sqrt{5+\sqrt{5}}}{4}\end{aligned}[/tex]

sin 57° = sin (75° - 18°) = sin 75° cos 18° - cos 75° sin 18°

Cari sin 75°

[tex]\begin{aligned}\sin 75^\circ&=\sin(45^\circ+30^\circ)\\&=\sin 45^\circ\cos 30^\circ+\cos 45^\circ\sin 30^\circ\\&=\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}+\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\frac{1}{2}\\&=\frac{\sqrt{6}+\sqrt{2}}{4}\end{aligned}[/tex]

Cari cos 75°

[tex]\begin{aligned}\cos 75^\circ&=\cos(45^\circ+30^\circ)\\&=\cos 45^\circ\cos 30^\circ-\sin 45^\circ\sin 30^\circ\\&=\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}-\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\frac{1}{2}\\&=\frac{\sqrt{6}-\sqrt{2}}{4}\end{aligned}[/tex]

Hitung sin 57°

[tex]\begin{aligned}\sin 57^\circ&=\sin(75^\circ-18^\circ)\\&=\sin 75^\circ\cos 18^\circ-\cos 75^\circ\sin 18^\circ\\&=\frac{\sqrt{6}+\sqrt{2}}{4}~\frac{\sqrt{2}\sqrt{5+\sqrt{5}}}{4}-\frac{\sqrt{6}-\sqrt{2}}{4}~\frac{\sqrt{5}-1}{4}\\&=\frac{\left ( 2\sqrt{3}+2 \right )\sqrt{5+\sqrt{5}}}{16}-\frac{\sqrt{2}\left ( \sqrt{3}-1 \right )\left ( \sqrt{5}-1 \right )}{16}\\&=\frac{2\left ( \sqrt{3}+1 \right )\sqrt{5+\sqrt{5}}-\sqrt{2}\left ( \sqrt{3}-1 \right )\left ( \sqrt{5}-1 \right )}{16}\end{aligned}[/tex]
