9. 1. 20 kg/m3 = ..................... gram/cm3 2. 20 gram/cm3 =............... kg/m3 Pakai cara


1. 20 kg/m^3

#Cara biasa (desimal)

= 20 × 1000 gr / 1 × 100000 cm^3
= 20000 gr / 1000000 cm^3
= 0,02 gr/cm^3

#Cara pangkat :

20 kg/m^3 = 2 × 10 kg/m^3

= 2 × 10 × 10^3 gr / 1 × 10^6
= 2 × 10^4 gr / 10^6 cm^3
= 2× 10^-2 gr/cm^3

2. 20 gr/cm^3

#Cara biasa (desimal)

= 20 × 0,001 kg / 1 × 0,000001 m^3
= 0,02 kg / 0,000001 m^3
= 20000 kg/m^3

#Cara pangkat :

20 gr/cm^3 = 2 × 10 gr/cm^3

= 2 × 10 × 10-3 kg / 1 × 10^-6 m^3
= 2 × 10^-2 kg / 10^-6 m^3
= 2 × 10^4 kg/m^3

*Ket :
^ = pangkat
