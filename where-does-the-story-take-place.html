14. where does the dialog take place​


Jawaban:

Dialogue takes place when a character in a story talks. The easiest to spot dialogue is when it is a direct speech because this type of dialogue has quotation marks such as "this".

For example: " I love my mom," declared Sara.

However, dialogue could be a form of indirect speech where it doesn't use quotation marks. It generally start with who said it, then what they said.

For example: Sara declared that she loved her mom.
