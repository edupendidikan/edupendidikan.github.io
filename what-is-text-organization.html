1. What is text organization of Narrative text?​


Jawaban:

Text Organizations:

1. Orientation, which sets the scene and introduces the characters (it answers the questions: who, when, what and where)

2. Complication, in which a crisis or a problem arises, which usually involves the main characters

3. Resolution, a solution to the problem (for better o for worse). Here, the main characters find ways to solve the problem.


2. what is the text organization (text structure) in recount text


Recount Text  

Text organization of recount text:

Orientation: contains an introduction to the background of the story, when, where and the people who are involved in the story . (berisi tentang pengenalan mengenai latar belakang cerita, kapan , dimana dan orang-orang yang trerlibat di dalam cerita)Event: is a series of events in the story that are told chronologically . (merupakan rangkaian kejadian di dalam cerita yang di ceritakan secara kronologis)Reorientation: a conclusion which is the author's comments about the incident. (kesimpulan yang merupakan komentar penulis mengenai kejadian)

PEMBAHASAN:

Recount Text adalah salah satu jenis text dalam bahasa Inggris yang menceritakan kembali tentang kejadian-kejadian atau pengalaman-pengalaman di masa lampau. Tujuan dari Recount Text adalah untuk memberikan informasi atau untuk menghibur pembaca sehingga tidak terdapat konflik.

Unsur Kebahasaan:  

Menggunakan noun dan pronoun, contoh: Teuku Umar, weapons, soldiers, we, they  Menggunakan action verbs , contoh: went, played, spent  Menggunakan time conjunction, contoh: and, but, after, dan finally  Menggunakan past tense, contoh: they went to Surabaya  Menggunakan adverbs dan adverbs of phrases, contoh: in my house, two days ago, slowly  Menggunakan adjective, contoh: brave, strong, smart

apa tujuan, fitur bahasa dan organisasi teks dari teks deskriptif

Penjelasan:

DESCRIPTIVE TEXT║

ExplanationExplanation

Descriptive text merupakan teks yang menjabarkan suatu benda/orang/tempat dan hal lainnya secara spesifik.

SOCIAL FUNCTION/COMMUNICATIVE PURPOSE

to describe people/place/things specifically ( untuk mendeskripsikan orang/tempat/hal -hal secara khusus )

GENERIC STRUCTURE

{ Struktural teks deskripsi }

1. Identification

Bagian paragraf ini terletak di awal sebagai pengenalan siapa/apa yang akan dijabarkan dalam teks

2. Description

Bagian paragraf selanjutnya menjabarkan ciri khas-nya bagaimana dari orang/benda/tempat/hal yang di bicarakan.

LANGUAGE FEATURES

{ Unsur - unsur kebahasaan yang digunakan }

1. Penggunaan hal-hal spesifik yang dilibatkan dalam teks nya. { Specific nouns }

Example : my dream, my house, mount merapi

2. Penggunaan kata sifat ( adjective )

Kata sifat digunakan dalam penjabaran karakter khusus dari hal yang dibicarakan.

3. Penggunaan pola simple present tense

Dikarenakan sifat informasi nya berupa fakta, maka menggunakan pola simple present tense.

4. Penggunaan kata kerja aksi

Kata kerja aksi memuat informasi tindakan.

the basic purpose of the text narrative is to entertain and attract readers with a story or event that has the problem that lead to conflict and at the end of the story is no resolution or a happy ending, or even depressing. actual narrative text is not confined to the mystical story, fiction, legend, fairy tale or fable, but another story in the form of adventure, mystery and all forms of story. an essence, narrative text is about the story.

Generic structure of narrative text:

Orientation : Sets the scene: where and when the story happened and introduces the participants of the story: who and what is involved in the story. Complication : Tells the beginning of the problems which leads to the crisis (climax) of the main participants. Resolution : The problem (the crisis) is resolved, either in a happy ending or in a sad (tragic) ending Re-orientation/Coda : This is a closing remark to the story and it is optional. It consists  of  a  moral  lesson,  advice  or  teaching  from  the writer

Other basic components of a narrative are:

Plot - The events as they unfold in sequence. Characters - The people who inhabit the story and move it forward. Typically, there are minor characters and main characters. The minor characters generally play supporting roles to the main character, or the protagonist. Conflict - The primary problem or obstacle that unfolds in the plot that the protagonist must solve or overcome by the end of the narrative. The way in which the protagonist resolves the conflict of the plot results in the theme of the narrative. 26. 1. What is Descriptive Text? 2. What is the Purpose of Descriptive Text? 3. How many are there the organization of Descriptive Text? Explain


EXPLANATION

Descriptive text is a text which describes person, animal, place and other things specifically.

The communicative purpose of descriptive text is to describe people/place/things specifically

The descriptive text contains two organizations; identification and description.

~ Identification is the very beginning part of paragraph. It tells about the background, the introduction of who, what, which will be described in the text.

~ Description contains the quality or specific character which belongs to the things we are talking about.

LANGUAGE FEATURE

~ It refers to the specific participants to identify which the thing/the person is.

~ It uses the adjective more to explain the noun in detail.

~ It uses the simple present tense because it tells the fact or general truth

~ It uses the action verbs to reveal the activity
