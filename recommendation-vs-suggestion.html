“Recommendation is an official suggestion about the best thing to do, or the act of telling somebody that something is good or useful or that somebody would be suitable for a oarticular things, etc.”

Jadi recommendations adalah saran/himbauan kepada orang lain untuk melakukan hal yang terbaik atu berguna untuk orang tersebut tentang hal tertentu. Untuk rekomendasi di postingan kali ini sama halnya dengan memberikan advice (saran/nasihat) kepada orang lain sehingga orang lain tersebut sesuai dengan aturan atau keadaannya lebih baik

Recommendations Expressions / Ekspresi-Ekspresi Rekomendasi

Untuk memberikan rekomendasi/himbauan atau recommendations dalam bahasa Inggris kalian bisa menggunakan kata-kata di bawah ini:

1.     Should

2.     Keep away

Example of Recommendations Expressions / Contoh Kalimat Ekspresi Mengungkapkan Rekomendasi / Himbauan

ShouldYou should get up early and have more exercise. (Kamu harus bangun pagi dan lebih banyak berolahraga.)

You should try that new uniform. (Kamu harus mencoba seragam baru itu.)

Keep awayKeep away from the tigers. They are wild. (Menjauhlah dari harimau. Mereka itu hewan liar.)

35. buatlah dialog dengan kalimat kewajiban (obligation), larangan (prohibition), dan saran (suggestion/recommendation)! *pake bahasa inggris


Jawaban:

A: Hey, what are you doing?

B: Um, I'm throwing the rubbish. What's wrong?

A: Don't litter in this area.* Don't you see the warning board?

B: I'm sorry. I don't know.

A: You are supoosed to throw the rubbish in the trash can.^

B: Yes, It's my fault. I didn't mean to litter. I don't see the trash can, that's why.

A: You can find the trash can near the park. I suggest you to keep the rubbish in a plastic or in your bag until you find the trash can.° So you don't litter next time.

B: Okay, thank you for your suggestion and I'm verry sorry. I'll take the rubbish now.

A: No problem and that's a good idea. I appreciate it.

Penjelasan:

* kalimat prohibition (larangan)

^ kalimat obligation (kewajiban)

° kalimat suggestion (saran)
