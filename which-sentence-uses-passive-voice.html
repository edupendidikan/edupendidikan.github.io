17. 17. I'm reading a new novel. Which one is the passive voice form of the sentence above?​


Jawaban:

D. A new novel is being read by me.

Penjelasan:

Kalimat "I'm reading a new novel." menggunakan active voice dan present continuous tense, yang ditandai oleh penggunaan verb-ing, yang di sini adalah 'reading'. Struktur kalimat dengan active voice dan present continuous tense adalah sebagai berikut:

Subject + is/am/are + Verb-ing + Object.

I + am + reading + a new novel.

Oleh karena itu, passive voice dari kalimat tersebut harus juga menggunakan present continuous tense. Struktur kalimat dengan passive voice dan present continuous tense adalah sebagai berikut:

Object + is/am/are + being + Verb 3 + by + Subject

Maka, kalimatnya dalam passive voice menjadi:

A new novel + is + being + read + by + me

A new novel is being read by me.

Catatan:

Pada active voice, penggunaan is/am/are bergantung pada subject, sedangkan pada passive voice penggunaan is/am/are bergantung pada object.

22. passive voice uses:•pengertian•contoh​


Passive Voice Explanation

Definition and Function (Pengertian) :

Passive voice is when a sentence puts the direct object before the verb, and the subject after the verb.

Kalimat pasif atau passive voice digunakan untuk menunjukkan ketertarikan pada seseorang atau objek yang dikenai tindakan dan bukan seseorang atau objek yang melakukan tindakan.

Example (Contoh) :

Active Voice:

" Anna is carrying the fruit basket. "

Passive Voice:

" The fruit basket is being carried by Anna. "

29. Whice passive sentence uses past perfect tense


Jawaban:
My gown had been washed before I took a nap

Penjelasan:

Past Perfect Tense can be used to express an event that was completed at some time in the past before another event occurred.

Sentences Past Perfect Tense has the formula:

(active) S + had + Verb 3 + O

(passive) S + had + been + Verb 3 + by (agent)

From this formula, we can conclude that sentence B is a sentence that uses the Past Perfect Tense passive form because it has the following structure:

My gown (S) had been washed (Verb 3) before I took a nap. So the writer's dress had been washed first before she took a nap.

So the correct answer is B. My gown had been washed before I took a nap
