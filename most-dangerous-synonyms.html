5. Historical context of the most dangerous game ?


Jawaban:

General Zaroff’s Cossack background adds historical realism to the nightmarish hunt on Ship-Trap Island. Cossack is the general name given to a number of peoples in the Ural Mountains region of Eastern Europe in present-day Russia and the Ukraine. Many Russian Cossacks, such as Zaroff and his assistant, Ivan, were military specialists who fought for centuries as mercenary soldiers in service to the czar until the collapse of the Russian empire in 1917. The Cossacks played a crucial role in expanding Russia’s borders and maintaining order along the frontier. Deeply feared, Cossacks were infamous for their swift and violent attacks and use of guerilla warfare tactics. Although Connell had no intention of promoting stereotypes in his story, he nevertheless wanted to capitalize on the mythic violence of Cossack warfare to heighten dramatic tension and expose the calculated madness of a single monstrous persona. Connell’s extreme portrayal of the Cossacks thus provides a credible explanation for Zaroff’s otherwise unfathomable motivations for killing.

Penjelasan:

Latar belakang Cossack Jenderal Zaroff menambahkan realisme historis pada perburuan mimpi buruk di Pulau Perangkap Kapal. Cossack adalah nama umum yang diberikan kepada sejumlah orang di wilayah Pegunungan Ural di Eropa Timur di Rusia dan Ukraina saat ini. Banyak Cossack Rusia, seperti Zaroff dan asistennya, Ivan, adalah spesialis militer yang berjuang selama berabad-abad sebagai tentara bayaran yang melayani tsar sampai runtuhnya kekaisaran Rusia pada tahun 1917. Cossack memainkan peran penting dalam memperluas perbatasan Rusia dan mempertahankan ketertiban di sepanjang perbatasan. Sangat ditakuti, Cossack terkenal karena serangan mereka yang cepat dan kejam serta penggunaan taktik perang gerilya. Meskipun Connell tidak berniat mempromosikan stereotip dalam ceritanya, ia tetap ingin memanfaatkan kekerasan mitis perang Cossack untuk meningkatkan ketegangan dramatis dan mengekspos kegilaan yang diperhitungkan dari satu persona mengerikan. Penggambaran ekstrim Connell tentang Cossack dengan demikian memberikan penjelasan yang kredibel untuk motivasi pembunuhan Zaroff yang tidak terduga.

16. 1) most dangerous(superalative) .......(comparative) ..........(positive) 2) more expensive(comparative)..........(positive) ..........(superalative)​


Jawaban:

1) most dangerous (superalative)

more dangerous (comparative)

as dangerous as (positive)

2) more expensive (comparative)

as expensive as (positive)

most expensive (superalative)
