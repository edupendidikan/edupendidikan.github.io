32. Joko and jono (Buy) some food in the canteet school


Jawabannya :

SIMPLE PRESENT TENSE

Joko and Jono Buy some food in the canteet school

Karena Joko and Jono itu They (mereka). Sedangkan Simple present tense itu untuk She, He, It

Semoga MembantuANSWER
________

PRESENT TENSE
Joko and jono buy some food in the canteen school.
=> Joko Dan Jono adalah plural noun , Jadi tdk perlu ditambah suffix/akhiran-S/ES.

PAST TENSE
Joko and jono bought some food in the canteen school.
=> Buy di ubah ke Verb 2

PRESENT PERPECT TENSE
Joko and jono have bought some food in the canteen school.
