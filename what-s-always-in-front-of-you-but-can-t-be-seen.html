4. why__you always __(sleep) in front of the TV?​


Jawaban:Why DO you always SLEEP in front of the TV?

PEMBAHASAN Simple Present Tense

berfungsi untuk menunjukkan Peristiwa yg terjadi di masa sekarang (Present), Kegiatan berulang/rutin, kebiasaan (habitual) dan utk menyatakan kebenaran umum (fakta).

Ciri / waktu Present TenseEverydayEvery morningalways ✔️usually

Rumus Present Tense(+) S + V1 (-s/es) + O/C(-) S + Do/Does + not + V1 + O/C(?) Do/Does + S + V1 + O/C?

=> Subject + Auxiliary

I, you, we, they + do ✔️He, She, it + does

KESIMPULAN Kalimat ada “always” (selalu) yang menunjukkan bahwa Kegiatan “sleep” (tidur) dilakukan secara berulang. Maka kalimat tersebut Present Tense.Sesuai aturan Present Tense jika Subjek “you” maka menggunakan Auxiliary “do” dan kata kerja tetap Verb-1 “sleep”.

29. i ... so nervous to sing in front of you.(be)​


I am so nervous to sing in front of you.

PEMBAHASANSimple Present Tense

Yaitu bentuk tenses yang digunakan untuk menunjukkan suatu kejadian yang sedang berlangsung atau berulangkali.

RUMUS

Positive Sentences:

Subject + Verb 1 + ObjectSubject + to be + Object

Negative Sentences:

Subject + do/does + not + Verb 1 + ObjectSubject + to be + not + Object

Interrogative Sentences:

Do/does + Subject + Verb 1 + Object + ?To be + Subject + Object + ?

Based on Question

~ I (to be) so nervous to sing in front of you.

Setelah subjek terdapat to be yang rumpang. Setelah to be ada sifat (adjective). Maka kalimat tersebut berupa Present Simple.

I (to be) so nervous to sing in front of you.

➡ I am so nervous to sing in front of you.

Contoh:

She is very diligent.

Dira and Vira are beautiful.

In my class, Noah is a very smart student.
