6. Dari sekelompok data, diketahui meannya adalah 50. Jika sebuah data yg besarnya 48 dan angka bakunya -1, maka simpang baku nya adalah ?


Z = angka baku

X = data

X-bar = rata-rata

S = simpangan baku


Z = (X - X-bar)/S

-1 = (48-50) / S

S = -2/-1

S = 2

Inggris

RSVP is a French phrase that means "Please answer" to ask for confirmation of the invitation.

However, RSVP has rarely been used in confirmation invitations in France, so now this is considered formal and old-fashioned.

RSVP in English can also mean "Please respond". But this is not the true meaning in English.
