12. tulis ulang kalimat dalam kalimat pasif 1. we use mobiles every day 2. my brother pilots the airplane 3. he will publish the novel in a month 4. professional models teach young models 5. they serve lunch at 12.00 mohon bantuan nya !!


Subject : English
Class : X
Chapter : Passive voice
Keyword : present simple, simple future

Kode : 10.5.13 [Kelas 10 Bahasa Inggris Bab 13 - Passive Voice]


1. we use mobiles every day
✐ mobiles are used by us every day
2. my brother pilots the airplane
✐ the airplane is piloted by my brother
3. he will publish the novel in a month
✐ the novel will be published by him in a month
4. professional models teach young models
✐ young models are taught by professional models
5. they serve lunch at 12.00
✐ lunch is served by them at 12.00

Note :
• 1-5 menggunakan present simple tense, kecuali nomor 3 karena menggunakan simple future

Formulas :
• present simple : O + is/are + V3 (by S)
• simple future : O + will be + V3 (by S) ANSWER
_________

=> Active
We use mobiles every day
=> Passive
Mobiles are used by us every day

=> Active
My brother pilots the airplane
=> Passive
The airplane is piloted by my brother

=> Active
He will publish the novel in a month
=> Passive
The novel will be published by him in a month.

=> Active
professional models teach young models
=> Passive
Young models are taught by professional models.

=> Active
They serve lunch at 12.00
=> Passive
Lunch is served by them at 12.00

26. intraction models Rekayasa perangkat lunak​


Jawaban:

Abstrak

Rekayasa Perangkat Lunak menjadi kebutuhan intitusi,organisasi atau perusahaan dalam pembangunan sistem informasi, menjadi bagian penting yang melatarbelakangi tumbuhnya perkembangan perangkat lunak dengan berbagai krisis perangkat lunak menurut berbagai sisi pandang pengguna. Rendahnya motivasi belajar rekayasa perangkat lunak merupakan salah satu kompetensi yang harus dimiliki oleh Mahasiswa, tujuan penelitian ini dilakukan untuk implementasi rekayasa perangkat lunak dan penerapan methodelogi yang terdapat pada buku literatur. Model rekayasa perangkat lunak. Metodologi yang digunakan adalah Model pembelajaran rekayasa perangkat lunak berbasis multimedia yang dibuat melalui beberapa tahapan antara lain : Penelitian Pengembangan (research and development /R & D) Gall dan Borg, desain sistem pembelajaran (instructional system design/ISD), model pengembangan model pembelajaran motivasi (attention, relevance, confidient and relevance/ARCS). Sedangkan pengembangan multimedianya : siklus hidup pengembangan (sistem system development life cycle/SDLC) dan perancangan sistem berorientasi objek (object oriented analysis design/OOAD). Keluarannya adalah produk sistem pembelajaran manajemen proyek teknologi informasi berbasis multimedia yang akan dilaksanakan pada pembelajaran LMS dan CD interaktif pembelajaran. Selanjutnya pembelajaran ini dapat diterapkan pada pendidikan tinggi di Indonesia.

34. Evaluate the techniques and models used by Ptolemy and Copernicus to explain the setup of the universe.


Jawaban:

Ptolemy believed in the theory that the earth was the center of the universe (geocentric) and it is not moving in the center while Copernicus later proposed otherwise, in his heliocentric theory he believed that the sun was the center of the universe but it was not moving. Both theories were influenced by their observation of how the light shade from the sun changes during the day, and also through observation of other celestial bodies whose light intensity changes which made them arrived to the conclusion that they are not equidistant nor stationary with respect to earth.
